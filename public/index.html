Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how Crowd Trust works? Find answers to some of the most common inquiries below.

What is Crowd Trust?
Crowd Trust is a platform that connects individuals and organizations to foster trust and transparency in community-driven projects.
How can I participate?
You can participate by joining our campaigns, contributing to projects, or simply spreading the word.
Is there a fee to join?
No, joining Crowd Trust is completely free. We believe in open access to empower communities.
How do I start a campaign?
Simply create an account and navigate to the campaign creation section. Follow the guided steps to launch your project.
Can I donate to campaigns?
Yes, you can support campaigns by donating directly through the platform. Every contribution counts!
How can I contact support?

Where can I find more information?
For more information, check our About section or visit our Campaigns page.
